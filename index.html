How to get your Personal Access Token
  1. Go to id.getharvest.com/developers and sign in
  2. Under Personal Access Tokens, click Create new personal access token
  3. Give it any name (e.g. "Hours Tracker") and click Create
  4. Copy the token — it only displays once
  5. Your Harvest Account ID is shown on that same page under Your active accounts
  6. Your Forecast Account ID is found at forecastapp.com/settings under Integrations, or in the URL when logged in (e.g. forecastapp.com/123456/settings)
